Cookie Policy

Understanding how we use cookies to improve your experience

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our website. They help us recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your preferences and actions.

Cookies are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and to provide information to website owners.

2 Types of Cookies We Use

3 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To keep you logged in and secure your account
  • Preferences: To remember your settings and preferences
  • Security: To protect against unauthorized access and fraud
  • Analytics: To understand how our website is used and improve our services
  • Performance: To ensure our website loads quickly and efficiently

4 Third-Party Cookies

We may use third-party services that set their own cookies. These include:

  • Payment Processors: Razorpay and PayPal for secure payment processing
  • Analytics: To help us understand website usage patterns

We do not control these third-party cookies. Please refer to the respective third-party privacy policies for more information.

5 Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies in several ways:

Browser Settings

Most browsers allow you to refuse cookies or delete existing ones. Here's how:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
  • Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

Opt-Out

You can opt out of analytics cookies by contacting us or adjusting your browser settings. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect website functionality.
